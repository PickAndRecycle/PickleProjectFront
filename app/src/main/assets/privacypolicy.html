Date created: December 11, 2015

Pick and Recycle is an app for raising awareness of waste management to the society so that we can create a better place to live and keeping the environment clean for everyone. By using our app, you can post your trash or unused belongings that may be useful for others, so that other users can either pick your trash and send it over to waste banks, or even reuse the unused goods. This app is intended specifically in Jakarta region.

When you are using our services, you will share several informations with us. Therefore, by reading this policy, you will have a clear information on how we handle your data. Feel free to contact us if you have any questions.

Information We Collect

By using our app, you are sharing information to us, such as:

1. Information that you choose to give us

Pick and Recycle app requires users to set an account before any services can be done, such as your username, password, email address, and telephone number. This can also be done by registering via Google Plus account.

2. Information that we get automatically when you are using our services

  1. Usage Information

    2. We collect information regarding on your activity whenever you are using Pick and Recycle services.

  2. Content Information

    3. We monitor all of the trash activities that is done by users, such as the photo of each trash, details of the trash (category, quantity, condition, username of the trash picker, and also your username every time you have inserted a new trash to our database).

  3. Device Information

    • We are using your device information in order to detect any crashes and issues during your experience of using our app.
    • We are also using your mobile phone information so that others who picked your trash able to contact you and set an agreement between the trash picker and the trash thrower.

  4. Camera

    5. “Throw trash” feature in our app requires users to access the device’s camera in order to ease other users for your trash information. “Report” feature also use the same access so that the government can easily handle the trash management issues.

  5. Location Information

    6. Within our app, it is essential to share your location information because our app mainly focuses on where you are located at the moment. This will ease the feature to check any trash that is located nearby, and it is also essential for tracking the thrower’s location whenever someone picks the particular user’s trash.

3. Information that we collect from third parties

Pick and Recycle app integrates with Google Plus accessibility so that user would not have to fill in the registration form, but just easily connect their account and use our app instantly. We also provide social media sharing whenever you have posted a new trash to our app. In this case, we collect information through these third parties sources and use it to fill in the required information in our app.

How We Use Information

Based on the things that we have stated above, we use your information to:

  • Develop our application
  • Monitor your usage
  • Verify your identity

We also store several information locally on your device, so that you would not have to load it from the internet. This makes the content to load faster.

How We Share Information

We may share your information to other users so that they can easily contact you when they would like to pick up your trash. We also share it among our developers to monitor your activities.

Revisions to the Privacy Policy

There might be some changes to our privacy policy from time to time. We will let you know by changing the revision date of the existing privacy policy.
