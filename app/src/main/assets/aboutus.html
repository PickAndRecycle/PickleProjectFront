About Us

PICKLE, which stands for Pick and Recycle, is a startup created by Computer Science students of University Of Indonesia with the help and association with Nostra Technology Company. Seeing that trash has became one major problem in Jakarta and it remains an unsolved one, we want to tried to make a new solution for this problem. To solve this problem, we want the society also to contribute to our project for a better Jakarta.

Pickle believes that by the help of this application, local citizens can have a new insight towards the danger of waste and help to contribute by managing waste properly. We also hoped that this project can help the life of people who contributed and makes our environment better.
